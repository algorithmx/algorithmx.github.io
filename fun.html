Fun Projects

Below is a list of topics or projects I have been involved or I am interested. Given my frequent engagement in simulation projects, I have cultivated a 'visual debugging' methodology. where I highly prioritize the visualization of intermediate results in the development process. By juxtaposing visualization with my physical intuition, I am usually able to quickly identify many kind of mistakes.

To help me get over the bitter time after a sudden layoff, I build visualization pages to explain the essence of the listed topic. Please click on the title to access the visualization page.

Kinetic Monte-Carlo diffusion simulation

Event-based simulation of particle diffusion on a 2D honeycomb lattice using Kinetic Monte-Carlo methods. Demonstrates diffusion dynamics through site occupation states and hopping rules.
