About Mangata & Gallo

At Mangata & Gallo, we believe that jewelry is more than just adornment; it's a vessel for memories, a symbol of love, and a celebration of life's most precious moments. Our mission is to craft pieces that resonate with the heart, capturing the essence of engagements, weddings, and anniversaries with unparalleled elegance and craftsmanship.

Our story began in the vibrant city of Austin, Texas, where our founder, Mariana, a cherished local designer, embarked on a journey to bring her vision to life. With a deep-rooted passion for diamond cutting and metal smithing, honed through years of dedicated study, Mariana has infused each piece with a touch of her soul, ensuring that every creation is not just seen but felt.

Though our physical store is a cozy corner in Austin, our heart reaches out to the world through our online presence. Here, we invite you into our atelier, a space where you can explore, dream, and find the perfect piece that speaks to you. Each visit is an opportunity to connect, to share in the joy of discovery, and to celebrate the milestones that define our lives.

Mariana's commitment to her craft is unwavering. She has nurtured Mangata & Gallo from a single spark of inspiration to a beacon of tradition and innovation. Recently, she has welcomed skilled artisans into our fold, each adding their unique touch to her designs, and a vibrant young mind to manage our digital presence, ensuring that our story continues to unfold with warmth, creativity, and a personal touch.
